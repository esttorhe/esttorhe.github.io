## The Platform Dilemma
- You see the architectural needs (consolidation, DX), but have zero formal authority over product teams.
- Seen this at Spotify (Backstage) and Zenjob.

## Strategies to drive adoption
- Solve immediate pain: sell them on shipping faster, not architectural purity.
- Build coalitions: find champion senior engineers in product domains.
- Treat platform as a product: if you need a CTO mandate, your DX is probably terrible.
