## The Paradox
- New managers delay hard conversations to "protect" the team.
- It feels like kindness, but it's actually cruel to the report.

## My Experience
- Early career: failed to give direct feedback early. Led to worse outcomes.
- The Marius quote: "I thought I was being nice, but it was actually horrible."

## Key Takeaways to expand on
- Clear is kind.
- Delaying is about protecting the manager's comfort, not the report.
- Need to detail how structured PIP cycles fix this.
