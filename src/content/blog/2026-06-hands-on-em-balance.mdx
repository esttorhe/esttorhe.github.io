## The Debate
- Should EMs write code? Yes, but not on the critical path.
- Ivory tower managers lose touch with the team.

## Where to apply hands-on skills
- Code for context, not for velocity (understand DX, deployment friction).
- Be the ultimate unblocker: take the gnarly tech debt or ambiguous architecture tasks.
- Lead from the trenches: earn respect by pairing with juniors when under pressure.
